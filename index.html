About Turner

Hello! I'm Turner. My journey into holistic healing began, as many do, with my own path of transformation. I fell in love with exploring my inner landscape through a disciplined practice of Yoga, Reiki, Meditation, Pranayama, and Shipibo Plant Dietas. These disciplines became my greatest allies while navigating personal obstacles with anxieties, addictions, depression, and disconnection. My practice is trauma-informed by lived experience. I hold space for you to transmute pain into presence, honoring your wounds as pathways to wholeness.

My work is inspired by a lifelong immersion in these practices—from the stillness of Zazen at the Upaya Zen Center; the discipline of Sivananda Hatha Yoga at Yogi Hari's Ashram; to the profound mystical healing traditions of the indigenous Shipibo of Peru. I have lived within these cultures to bring you embodied teachings. Since discovering Reiki in 2020, I have deepened my practice under the guidance and inspiration of my mentor, Valerie Brodar.

Today, I channel the powerful alignment of these traditions to provide a balanced, supported, and guided session for you. My life is dedicated to these healing arts, and I am passionate about sharing them with you.
